Centrica

O365 Consultant / Architect

Energy is undergoing a revolution. The way we use it, the way we control it, and the way we access it are all changing.

In the future, customers will receive a more personalised, hassle-free service, that enables lower bills while also delivering low carbon energy.

Technology is a key driver of this change, because it will transform how customers interact with energy suppliers and will shift their expectations.

For their part, energy suppliers will need to evolve beyond traditional roles focused on commodities and billing, to enable new types of energy markets and increasingly become a trusted service partner for homes and businesses.

But while this energy transition is opening up exciting possibilities, it won’t be without its challenges. A new paper by Centrica and KPMG seeks to explore what these challenges are, how they can be tackled, and what the future of energy will look like. As new technologies and sector convergence integrate energy into other aspects of our lives, how will this revolution shape the UK energy market and change customer experience?
